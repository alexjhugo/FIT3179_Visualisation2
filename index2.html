TOKYO 2020 SUMMER OLYMPICS

The Tokyo 2020 Summer Olympics is an international sports event that was long awaited by many countries, since the last summer olympic games in Brazil, 2016. It was the fourth olympic games to be held in Japan, set in their capital city of Tokyo. It was also unfortunately delayed by a year due to the emergence of the COVID-19 Pandemic that caused mass lockdowns around the world. However, the games eventually took place and through this visualisation, we'll discuss about some of the event's key highlights.
